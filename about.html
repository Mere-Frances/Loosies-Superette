Our Story

We pride ourselves in good service and providing our customers with a sensory overload of pop-culture memorabilia and lights. We promote our new content with affordable and fun deals, ranging from cocktails to apple-pies.

We have a separate saloon area in the basement with a proper bar and pool tables, which is available for private bookings or functions.

Our manager Jack Carter (seen in photo), an Englishman, is friendly and gregarious, always happy to sit down and have a chat.

“Unique interior, familiar food, welcoming service, exclusive to
Wellington but with all the good parts of casual dining chains overseas.”

Our Food

Check out our $10 cheeseburgers & hot dogs everyday from 10pm

We serve a variety of generous diner meals that go down a treat any time of day or night.

After something more gourmet? we have that too! like our hot-buttered cheesy garlic loaf and steaks! Can’t forget about that sweet tooth? try our famous deep-fried New York Cheesecake or a sweet cocktail.

We try our absolute best but unfortunately our kitchen is not a gluten-free zone, so please chat to our team if you have any questions.

Our Beverages

Cocktail Hour Everyday 6-7pm & 9-10pm

Featured cocktails : Dirty Shirley Temple, Pickled Chilli Margo, Banana Daiquiri, the Jelly Tip Espresso Martini

We’ve got a cocktail for any occasion! And if you’re the sober-driver, we got you! Just ask any of our friendly bartenders and they’ll be happy to make it a mocktail instead, or list our non-alcoholic sodas and juices.

Our alcoholic beverages are only available for those over 18, and please don’t be offended if we ask for your ID, it means you look good for your age! We are only allowed to accept NZ Drivers Licenses, 18+ Kiwi Access cards, or valid passports.

Pool, DJ, Functions & More

We have many services available at our unique venue:

  • Pool tables for all customers to use ($2 coin per game)
  • Speakeasy downstairs available for hire
  • DJ’s on every Friday & Saturday from 7pm (dance floor available)
  • Daily deals - happy hour, after 10pm food deals, new feature specials, Sunday specials, etc

If you’re interested in any of our awesome services, come on up to Cuba street and give us a visit! Or contact us for more details or to make a booking!
